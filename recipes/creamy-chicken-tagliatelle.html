Creamy Chicken Tagliatelle

Description

Creamy chicken tagliatelle is a comforting italian-inspired pasta dish made with cubed tender chicken breast tossed in a rich, velvety cream sauce and served over silky ribbons of tagliatelle. Enhanced woth galric, Parmesan cheese and herbs, it delivers a perfect balance of savory, creamy and satisfying flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 350g tagliatelle
  • 1 kg chicken breast
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cups cream
  • 2 tablespoons parsley
  • Grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook the tagliatelle according to packet instructions, or untill al dente. Drain, reserving 1-2 cups of pasta water.
  2. Heat a frying pan on high heat.
  3. Dice and season chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. Once hot, add the chicken to the heated frying pan. Cook untill all sides are a light golden brown color, stirring regularly (Approximately 4-5 minutes - take care to not overcook the chicken!).
  5. Turn the heat down to low. After 1 minute, add the chopped garlic and cook for another minute. Take care to not burn the garlic!
  6. Add the cream and turn the heat up to medium. Make sure to scrape up all the fond, thats where all the flavor is!
  7. When the cream is just starting to bubble, add the drained tagliatelle and pasta water untill the sauces consistency is to your liking.
  8. Turn off the heat and scatter with parsly. Stir well and adjust seasoning to taste.
  9. Serve with grated parmesan on top.
